DIVING CLUB.

The Fidelia Diving Club on Phu Quoc is located 2 minutes from the Pier in Duong Dong. Diving in Phu Quoc’s warm, shallow and calm waters are just perfect for snorkeling. We offer daily diving and snorkeling to north and south Phu Quoc. Go underwater or stay at the surface to enjoy the island’s reef and marine life.
